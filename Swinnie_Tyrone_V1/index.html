The Latest News

Coming to the Falcons franchise together as rookies playing the same position, it was a safe assumption that cornerbacks Desmond Trufant and Robert Alford would be close after being drafted in the first (Trufant) and second (Alford) rounds of this year's draft. Though they experienced vastly different football worlds in college with Trufant playing in the Pac-12 at Washington and Alford at small-school Southeastern Louisiana, their relationship began long before becoming roommates and Atlanta Falcons.  Read More


The Atlanta Falcons and USA Football will team up to instruct Atlanta-area youth coaches on Heads Up Football player safety initiatives. Former Atlanta Falcons linebacker and USA Football Master Trainer, Buddy Curry, will conduct a Heads Up Football Player Safety Coach clinic on Saturday, July 13 at Marist High School in Atlanta, GA. Curry will instruct more than 30 Player Safety Coaches representing leagues from throughout the state of Georgia. This group will then teach Heads Up Football to their youth league coaches, players and parents.  Read More


Many Falcons fans are anxiously awaiting the first appearance of Steven Jackson in the Falcons' offense. While training camp and the preseason creep towards us, fans may have to wait a little longer to see much of No. 39 in red and black. While speaking with the media during May's OTAs, offensive coordinator Dirk Koetter provided a hint on what to expect from Jackson during the four-game exhibition season: not a lot. Read More
